Why us


For over a decade, Rela has been an integral part of Chisinau's nightlife, situated in the vibrant city of Moldova. As a longstanding establishment, we take pride in our contribution to shaping the local underground scene and fostering a dynamic environment for music enthusiasts.

Founded in the heart of Chisinau, Moldova, Rela emerged in 2019 and has since been a beacon of support for both local and international DJs. Our stage has hosted renowned artists, including 50 Cent, Ariana Grande and DJ Khalid, creating an inclusive space that pulsates with energy every weekend.

Our architect, with a rich history in global club scenes since adolescence, has left an indelible mark on the nightlife landscape. Hailing from London, France, LA, and a fixture in Vancouver's scene since the 1980s, they are the visionary behind our iconic sound system and the assembly of a team featuring world-class visual and performance artists. The Rela family consists of passionate individuals who grew up in the club culture, dedicated to preserving the values it represents.

Derived from the name of the club's founder, "Rela" captures the mysterious essence of twilight, inviting all to lose or find themselves in this transformative space. Step into Rela, where Chisinau's nightlife pulses with energy—a haven for music, art, and self-discovery.
